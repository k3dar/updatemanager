Debian Jessie is now stable

April 25, 2015
Debian has announced that Jessie is the new stable release and with that SolydXK too will move from testing to stable.
If you were using the January ISO or you already upgraded your system to the last SolydXK Update (2015.01.15), you don't have to do anything at all: your system is already configured to use the Debian Jessie repositories.

If you haven't upgraded your system yet, these topics were made available on our forum if you need support:
HE to Jessie upgrade
BE to Wheezy upgrade
BE to Jessie upgrade
